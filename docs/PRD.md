# 📄 Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Title

**Hidden Gem – Community Driven Local Business Discovery Platform**

---

# 1. Product Overview

Hidden Gem is a **community-driven platform** designed to help small local businesses gain digital visibility. Many neighborhood businesses such as tailors, repair shops, street food vendors, and family-run shops do not have an online presence. As a result, they remain invisible to the younger generation who rely on online searches to discover services.

The proposed system allows users to **discover, review, and promote local “hidden gem” businesses** in their area. It also provides a **simple onboarding system for business owners**, enabling them to create a digital storefront in under two minutes using minimal information such as name, location, and a photo.

The platform bridges the **digital gap between local businesses and modern consumers**, helping local vendors reach new customers while allowing communities to highlight and support local services.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many small local businesses lack the technical knowledge, time, or resources to establish an online presence. As a result, they are not visible on popular platforms such as search engines or digital maps.

At the same time, modern consumers rely heavily on digital platforms to discover services and shops. This creates a gap where many useful and authentic local services remain undiscovered.

A simple and accessible platform is needed to **digitally represent these businesses and connect them with nearby customers.**

---

# 3. Target Users

### Customers

People who want to discover useful local businesses and services near them.

### Local Business Owners

Small vendors who want a **quick and simple way to create an online presence** without technical skills.

### Community Contributors

Users who can help add and promote hidden local businesses in their area.

---

# 4. Key Features

## 1. Hidden Gem Discovery Map

Users can explore nearby local businesses through a **map-based interface** that displays pinned locations.

Features include:

* Location-based discovery
* Nearby business suggestions
* Category filters

---

## 2. Simple Business Onboarding Wizard

Business owners can create a digital storefront within **2 minutes**.

Required details:

* Business Name
* Category
* Location
* Photo
* Short description

Once submitted, the system automatically creates a **digital business page**.

---

## 3. Business Profile Page

Each business will have a dedicated page containing:

* Business name
* Photos
* Location
* Category
* Description
* User reviews

---

## 4. Community Reviews and Ratings

Users can share their experiences by:

* Rating businesses
* Writing reviews
* Recommending services

This helps improve trust and visibility for good local businesses.

---

## 5. Smart Search and Filters

Users can easily find businesses using filters such as:

* Category
* Location
* Ratings
* Popularity

---

# 6. User Flow

## Business Owner Flow

1. Open the platform
2. Click **Add Business**
3. Enter business details
4. Upload photo
5. Submit form
6. Digital storefront is created

---

## User Discovery Flow

1. User opens the platform
2. Map displays nearby hidden businesses
3. User selects a business
4. Views details and reviews
5. Leaves a review or rating

---

# 7. Success Metrics

Success of the system can be evaluated using:

* Number of businesses onboarded
* Number of user reviews
* Daily active users
* Number of searches and discoveries
